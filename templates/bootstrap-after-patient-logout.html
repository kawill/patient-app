About

CliniScheduler is a web application that provides a quick and convenient way to request clinic visits online. Patient/provider communication is improved as patients may access personal notes and physician’s orders regarding their medical concerns at their convenience.


Easily request clinic visits online.

Access advice, information and orders from the Provider.

Record notes prior to, during and after clinic visits.

Access a log of prior and future visits.
